Privacy Policy for Liquids

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

Liquids is a freemium hydration tracking app that respects your privacy. We collect minimal information necessary to provide our services. Data collection varies between the free version (with ads) and premium version (ad-free):

Data You Provide:

  • Hydration Data: Drink entries, custom drinks, daily goals, and preferences
  • Account Information: When you sign in with Google, we store your Google account ID for cloud synchronization
  • Settings: Reminder preferences, notification settings, and app customizations

Data We Do NOT Collect Directly:

  • Personal identification information beyond Google account ID
  • Precise location data or GPS coordinates
  • Contact lists or phone data
  • Health data beyond hydration tracking
  • Sensitive personal information

Additional Data Collection (Free Version Only):

  • Advertising Data: Google AdMob collects advertising identifiers and usage data for ad personalization
  • General Location: Approximate location may be used for regional ad targeting
  • Device Information: Device model, OS version, and screen specifications for ad optimization

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used for:

  • App Functionality: Tracking your hydration progress and displaying analytics
  • Cloud Synchronization: Syncing your data across your devices via Google Firebase
  • Personalization: Saving your preferences and custom drinks
  • Safety Features: Providing alcohol consumption warnings
  • Advertising (Free Version Only): AdMob uses collected data to display relevant advertisements
  • Service Improvement: Anonymous usage patterns help improve app features

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Your data is primarily stored locally on your device
  • Cloud Storage: When you sign in with Google, data is securely stored in Google Firebase with encryption
  • Third-Party Access: We do not share, sell, or provide your hydration data to third parties. However, AdMob (free version only) may share advertising data with ad partners as outlined in Google's policies
  • Secure Transmission: All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • Data Processing Location: Data may be processed in the United States and other countries where Google operates

4. Your Rights and Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: View all your data within the app
  • Export: Your data can be exported from the app
  • Delete: Sign out to stop cloud sync, or delete the app to remove all local data
  • Opt-out: Use the app without signing in to avoid cloud storage entirely
  • Ad Personalization Control: Disable ad personalization in your device settings (Android: Google Settings > Ads)
  • Premium Upgrade: Upgrade to Premium to eliminate all ad-related data collection
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format

5. Data Retention

  • Local Data: Stored until you delete the app or clear app data
  • Cloud Data: Stored until you sign out or request deletion
  • Account Deletion: Contact us to permanently delete cloud data
  • Advertising Data: AdMob retains advertising data according to Google's retention policies (typically 2 years)
  • Analytics Data: Anonymous usage data may be retained for up to 2 years for service improvement

6. Children's Privacy

Liquids is suitable for users 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided data, please contact us immediately.

6a. Consent and User Choice

By using the free version of Liquids, you consent to:

  • AdMob Data Collection: Collection of advertising data as described in this policy
  • Personalized Ads: Display of personalized advertisements based on your interests
  • Data Sharing: Sharing of advertising data with Google's ad partners

You can withdraw consent at any time by:

  • Upgrading to the Premium version (eliminates all ad-related data collection)
  • Disabling ad personalization in your device settings
  • Uninstalling the app

7. Advertising and Free Version

Liquids offers both a free version with ads and a premium ad-free version:

Free Version with Ads:

  • Google AdMob: We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in the free version
  • Ad Personalization: AdMob may collect advertising identifiers and show personalized ads based on your interests
  • Data Collection: Google may collect device information, usage data, and advertising identifiers for ad serving
  • Third-Party Data: Ad networks may share data with advertising partners as outlined in Google's Privacy Policy
  • Opt-Out: You can limit ad tracking through your device settings or upgrade to Premium

Premium Version:

  • Ad-Free Experience: No advertisements or ad-related data collection
  • No Tracking: No advertising identifiers or behavioral tracking
  • Enhanced Privacy: Only essential data collection for app functionality

8. Third-Party Services

  • Google Play Services: For app distribution and payments (Google's Privacy Policy applies)
  • Google AdMob (Free Version Only): For displaying advertisements in the free version (Google's AdMob Privacy Policy applies)

Important: This data collection only occurs in the free version. Premium users experience no ad-related data collection.
