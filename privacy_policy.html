Privacy Policy for Liquids

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

Liquids is a premium hydration tracking app with no ads that respects your privacy. We collect minimal information necessary to provide our advanced features:

Data You Provide:

  • Hydration Data: Drink entries, custom drinks, daily goals, hydration preferences, and intake history
  • Account Information: When you sign in with Google, we store your Google account ID for cloud synchronization
  • Settings & Preferences: Reminder schedules, notification settings, language preferences, theme choices, and app customizations
  • Gamification Data: Achievement progress, daily challenge completions, streak records, and experience points
  • Custom Content: User-created drinks, custom cup sizes, favorite drink selections, and personalized goals

Device Data We Access:

  • Device Orientation: Gyroscope data for 3D water drop visualization (processed locally, not stored)
  • Notification Permissions: To deliver smart hydration reminders
  • Storage Access: For local data storage and export functionality (PDF/CSV)

Data We Do NOT Collect:

  • Personal identification information beyond Google account ID
  • Precise location data or GPS coordinates
  • Contact lists or phone data
  • Health data beyond hydration tracking
  • Sensitive personal information
  • Advertising identifiers (we have no ads)
  • Browsing history or external app usage

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used exclusively for providing and improving our services:

  • Core App Functionality: Tracking your hydration progress, displaying 3D visualizations, and generating comprehensive analytics
  • AI-Powered Insights: Analyzing your hydration patterns to provide personalized tips and behavior recommendations (processed locally)
  • Gamification Features: Managing achievements, daily challenges, progress levels, and streak tracking
  • Cloud Synchronization: Syncing your data across your devices via Google Firebase when you sign in
  • Smart Reminders: Scheduling and delivering personalized hydration notifications based on your preferences
  • Personalization: Saving your custom drinks, cup sizes, favorite beverages, and app preferences
  • Export & Analytics: Generating PDF reports and CSV data exports of your hydration history
  • Multi-Language Support: Displaying the app in your preferred language (13 languages supported)
  • Safety Features: Providing alcohol consumption warnings and hydration recommendations

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Your hydration data, achievements, and preferences are primarily stored locally on your device using encrypted databases
  • Cloud Storage: When you sign in with Google, data is securely stored in Google Firebase with end-to-end encryption
  • AI Processing: All AI insights and pattern analysis are processed locally on your device - no hydration data is sent to external AI services
  • No Third-Party Sharing: We do not share, sell, rent, or provide your personal or hydration data to any third parties
  • No Advertising Networks: Since we have no ads, no data is shared with advertising platforms or trackers
  • Secure Transmission: All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption and Firebase security protocols
  • Data Processing Location: Data may be processed in the United States and other countries where Google Firebase operates
  • Export Security: PDF and CSV exports are generated locally and stored securely on your device

4. Your Rights and Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Full Access: View all your hydration data, achievements, and settings within the app
  • Easy Export: Export your complete hydration history as PDF reports or CSV files for external analysis
  • Complete Deletion: Sign out to stop cloud sync, or delete the app to remove all local data permanently
  • Offline Usage: Use the app entirely offline without signing in to avoid any cloud storage
  • Data Portability: All exported data is in standard formats (PDF/CSV) for easy transfer to other apps
  • Selective Sync: Choose what data to sync to the cloud through your account settings
  • Immediate Control: Turn off reminders, disable features, or reset achievements at any time

5. Data Retention

  • Local Data: Stored until you delete the app or clear app data
  • Cloud Data: Stored until you sign out or request deletion
  • Account Deletion: Contact us to permanently delete cloud data
  • Analytics Data: Anonymous usage data may be retained for up to 2 years for service improvement

6. Children's Privacy

Liquids is suitable for users 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided data, please contact us immediately.

7. Third-Party Services

We use only essential, privacy-focused services:

  • Google Firebase: For authentication and secure cloud storage only (Google's Privacy Policy applies)
  • Google Play Services: For app distribution, updates, and payment processing (Google's Privacy Policy applies)
  • Google Play Billing: For processing one-time Premium purchases (price varies by country)
  • No Analytics Services: We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Analytics, or similar tracking services
  • No Advertising Networks: No AdMob, Facebook Ads, or any advertising platform integrations
  • No Crash Reporting: We do not use external crash reporting services that might collect additional data

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred and processed internationally:

  • Cloud Data: Google Firebase stores data on servers worldwide with appropriate security measures
  • Legal Protections: Data transfers comply with GDPR, CCPA, and other applicable privacy laws
  • Safeguards: Google maintains appropriate technical and organizational measures for secure international transfers
